Meet TresMarias & Prince

TRESMARIAS & PRINCE Milk Tea Shop is a family-run business in Mercedes, managed by twin sisters and their parents. Since opening in April 2023, we’ve been serving handcrafted milk teas, fruit teas, and snacks made with love and dedication. Every cup reflects their family’s passion for sharing happiness with the community.

How It All Began

The shop started with the twins’ curiosity and determination to learn milk tea making. Their first branch, “Bastikoy” in Sta. Elena, marked the beginning of their journey. With the support of their parents, they opened another store in Mercedes to bring simple yet affordable milk tea and snacks closer to more people.

Our Goal

To serve affordable, high-quality milk tea and snacks that bring comfort and joy to every customer, making each visit an enjoyable experience worth coming back for.

Our Dream

To grow as a beloved destination for milk tea lovers, not only in Mercedes but also in nearby communities-where every guest feels at home, and every cup reflects the family’s dedication, passion, and heart for service.
